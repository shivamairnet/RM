About Us

“Wherever you go, go with all your heart.” Nurtured from the idea to empower the traveller - RouteMaestro is a venture of Airnet Travels and Cargo Pvt. Ltd, pioneers in the travel and tourism industry of Ludhiana, Punjab. Founded in the year 1997, Airnet came to life to empower the Indian traveller. The company initiated its journey serving the Australia-India travel market offering a range of best-value products and services powered round-the-clock customer support and now have added other travel markets to their expertise. RouteMaestro.com is Airnet's vision to empower users and bring affordable flight booking solutions for Indian business and leisure travellers. Our support team is equipped with the necessary knkoweledge and the know-how's of the market. Airnet has been proactively diversifying into a variety of online and offline products and services.
